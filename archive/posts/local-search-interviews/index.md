Michael Gray, at Gray Wolf’s SEO Blog, conducted a large number of interviews with internet marketers on local search.

There are 20 different interviews, which will be published over the course of this week. I was one of the folks who participated in this series, and join Aaron Wall, Dean Bloomfield, and Scott Smith with some of my thoughts on Local Search.

I’m really enjoying the interviews published so far, and looking forward to the rest of the series.

Mike Blumenthal also has an excellent post from this weekend about the new changes to the way Google local onebox results appear, which is worth checking out: [Google Maps – Even more that is going on here](http://blumenthals.com/blog/2007/02/04/google-maps-even-more-that-is-going-on-here/)
