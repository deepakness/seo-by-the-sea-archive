Matt McGee, at Small Business SEM, asked Jon if he consider being the subject of an interview. Jon agreed. The first two parts are now online, and definitely worth a visit:

**Nonprofits**

A friend and former co-worker, Catherine Pancake, produced and directed a movie on mountaintop removal coal mining which is supposed to go into distribution this month. She’d be happy to discover that a group, ilovemountains.org, has created the [National Memorial for the Mountains](http://web.archive.org/web/20121007015101/http://ilovemountains.org/multimedia), which can be seen in Google Earth – and which received an [amazing amount of coverage](http://ilovemountains.org/news/627) in the press on Saturday.

Yuri Filimonov asked me if I would like to add some comments to his post on Charity web sites: creating, optimizing and promoting. I added some ideas, but the article was already filled with many great suggestions. Thanks, Yuri.

Google has started the Google Australia [Anita Borg](https://anitab.org/) scholarship this year, to encourage more women to get involved in computer science in Australia. Scholarships have been offered for women students in the US for the past three years, and for the first time in Australia this year.
