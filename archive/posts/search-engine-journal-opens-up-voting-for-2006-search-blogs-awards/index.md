Loren Baker sent me an email this morning letting me know that *SEO by the Sea* had been nominated as one of the selections for “SEO blog of the Year” alongside some tremendous company in Search Engine Journal’s [2006 Search Blogs Awards](https://www.searchenginejournal.com/vote-for-the-2006-search-blogs-awards/4161/).

Here are the different categories for the awards:

SEO Blog of the Year
Search News Blog
Search Marketing / Contextual Ad Blog
Best Link Building Blog
Best Search Agency Resource Blog
Best Search Engine Blogger of 2006
Best Social Media Optimization Blog
Best SEO Black Hat Blog(gers)
Best Local Search Blog
Best Affiliate Marketing Blog
Best Web 2.0 Blog
Best Search Engine Marketing Community / Forum

Thank you to those who nominated *SEO by the Sea*. Good luck to everyone who was nominated.

I’m really happy to see Cre8asite Forums has also been included in the Community/Forum nominees.
