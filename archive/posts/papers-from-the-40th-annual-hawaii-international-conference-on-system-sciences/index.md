The 40th Annual Hawaii International Conference on System Sciences (HICSS’07) was held on January 3-6, 2007, at the Hilton Waikoloa Village Resort, Waikoloa, Big Island, Hawaii.

It is a forum for an exchange of ideas in all areas of information sciences, and featured a very large number of presentations, and [papers](https://www.computer.org/csdl/proceedings/2007/hicss/12OmNBtl1zK) to accompany them. I’ve listed a few of those below that focus mostly upon online social networks and usability.
